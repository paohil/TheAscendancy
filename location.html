LOCATE THE ASCENDANCY

LOCATION

Vibrant, Colourful & Creative

With its cosmopolitan lifestyle, exciting cultural history and a city that is always alive, Brighton & Hove is a fascinating and diverse city outside of London. The Ascendancy being located in West Hove boasts strong commuter links to London, Brighton and the South Coast. Combined with its close proximity to the sea, The Ascendancy is an ideal choice for those who want the best of both worlds.

Local Highlights

  • Short walk to either Aldrington and Hove railway stations.
  • 1 hour 20 minute commute to London Victoria and 15 minutes to Brighton station.
  • Close to the main access roads serving London, Brighton and the South coast.
  • Neighbouring area populated with local boutiques, coffee shops, restaurants and national retail stores.
  • 10 minute stroll to the local beach.
  • The local primary school and nursery is on your doorstep.
